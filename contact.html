Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical home project take?

Home project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. New custom homes typically take 8-18 months, while renovations can range from 2-8 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.

What's included in your home design services?

Our comprehensive home design services include initial consultation, concept development, detailed floor plans, 3D home visualizations, construction documents, interior design, and project management support.

Do you work on homes outside Edinburgh?

Yes, we design homes throughout Scotland and the UK. For projects outside our local area, we can accommodate travel and coordinate with local contractors and professionals as needed.

What's your approach to sustainable home design?

Sustainability is integral to our home design philosophy. We incorporate energy-efficient systems, sustainable materials, and environmentally conscious practices in all our residential projects, with options for net-zero energy homes.
